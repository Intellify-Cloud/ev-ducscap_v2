## Logo Direction

The logo should communicate: **strength, trust, professionalism, forward movement**.

**Avoid:** Roof icons, keys, handshakes, dollar signs, houses — these are overused within real estate and finance.

### Recommended Logo Style

**Wordmark:** DUCES CAPITAL, supported by a geometric symbol.

**Possible icon concepts:**

- **Option A** — Circular form representing guidance, continuity, partnership
- **Option B** — Abstract upward movement representing growth, progress, financial advancement
- **Option C** — Stylised "D" representing direction, leadership, capital

---

## Photography Style

Use authentic imagery. Show:

- Real homeowners
- Property investors
- Modern homes
- Families
- Financial consultations
- South African property market

The imagery should feel **real, credible, and aspirational**.

---

## Visual Design Style

The design language should be: **clean, spacious, minimal, structured, elegant**.

**Characteristics:**
- Large typography
- White space
- Strong imagery
- Clear content hierarchy

**Avoid:** Clutter, excessive gradients, complex graphics, too many colours.

---

## Icon Style

Icons should be: **simple, thin line, modern**.

**Examples:** Home finance, bond approval, property investment, refinancing, mortgage advice.

**Avoid:** Cartoon icons.

## Website Design Direction

### Overall Feel

Think of a premium financial services firm rather than a bank. Visitors should immediately feel: *"These people know what they're doing."*

### Homepage Structure

#### Hero Section

- **Headline:** Property Finance Made Clear.
- **Subheading:** Helping homebuyers and property investors secure the right finance solution with confidence.
- **Primary CTA:** Apply Now
- **Secondary CTA:** Talk To An Advisor

#### Trust Section

Display: multiple lenders, years of experience, number of successful approvals, client testimonials.

#### Services Section

- Home Loans
- Bond Origination
- Refinancing
- Investment Property Finance
- Commercial Property Finance

#### Why Duces Capital

Simple 4-column section:
- Independent Advice
- Multiple Lender Access
- Expert Guidance
- Dedicated Support

#### Client Success Stories

Real testimonials.

#### Final CTA

Let's Find The Right Finance Solution For You.

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Professional, clear, reassuring, knowledgeable.

**Avoid:** Being salesy, corporate jargon, overly technical language.

### Key Messaging Themes

- **Trust** — "We help you make informed finance decisions."
- **Expertise** — "We understand the lending landscape."
- **Guidance** — "We walk with you throughout the process."
- **Results** — "We help secure competitive finance solutions."
